CricBorn

Cricket is not just a game but it’s more of emotion to billions of people unfurled worldwide. Amidst these billions, millions dream to make it big by playing this marvellous game and we are here to make it happen just concerning your dream.

CricBorn presents you with free online cricket courses by various national and international coaches worldwide. The best part of CricBorn is you may pursue as many video lectures through training sessions from various cricket coaches as you like. There is no limited tenure or limited intervals for watching the videos. These are meant to be lifelong.

According to the research of the International Cricket Council, India consists of 90% of One billion cricket fans. And among these billions of cricket fans, quite a lot of them aspires to become a cricketer one day. But the sad thing is not everyone meets the opportunities to become a successful cricketer. Therefore we came with this startup of Digital cricket coaching where our motto is to provide almost free coaching through training videos, lectures, webinars, quizzes, contests and any more. To be a part of all these, one can break the shackles of travelling long weary journeys and just have a good internet connection and a proper cricket kitbag to head to their dream destination.

"I’ve interviewed a lot of cricketers and heard stories of, rags to riches. I believe many people don’t meet the opportunities to head to their dream destiny and find themselves in despair. Therefore, CricBorn will help an aspiring cricketer to turn his dreams into destiny.”

Arijit Kundu
(Founder: CricBorn)
